## Response SLA

- First response target on PH comments: <= 15 minutes (08:00-20:00)
- Escalation SLA for bug/payment issue reports: <= 5 minutes acknowledgement

## Messaging rules

1. Thank first, answer clearly, ask one follow-up question.
2. Never argue in comments; convert tension into concrete next action.
3. Avoid over-claiming; share exact capability and current limits.

## Launch-day metrics to record

- PH upvotes by time block
- Number of comments and average first-response time
- Sign-up starts
- Checkout starts
- Paid conversions (if any)
- Top feedback themes

## End-of-day output

Append one summary block to `SESSION_LOG.md` with:

1. What worked
2. What failed
3. Immediate fixes for next 24h
